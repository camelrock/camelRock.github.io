Realizing now that I haven't actually defined 'camelCase'... basically, the first word is all in lowercase, with all subsequent words having an UPPERCASE first letter and any further letters being lowercase - eg : camelCase.

so 'HomePage' is almost regular camelCase

but if you look carefully, it starts with an UPPERCASE letter - which basically means it isn't

That style is in fact called 'PascalCase' - or sometimes 'upperCamelCase' (I'm guessing the term 'UpperCamelCase' is a bit like saying "soccer" outside the United States of America)
